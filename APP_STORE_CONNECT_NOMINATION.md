## Tips for Filling Out the Form

1. **Be Specific** - Mention "Apple Intelligence" and "Foundation Models API"
2. **Emphasize Privacy** - This aligns with Apple's values
3. **Show Impact** - Explain the problem you solve
4. **Highlight Timing** - Apple Intelligence just launched
5. **Be Concise** - Stay within character limits
6. **Proofread** - No typos or errors
7. **Be Honest** - Don't exaggerate

---

## After Submission

**What to Expect:**
- Response time: 2-4 weeks (or no response)
- No response doesn't mean rejection
- They may feature you months later
- Keep improving your app regardless

**Follow Up:**
- Don't follow up for at least 4 weeks
- If you make significant updates, resubmit
- Keep building and marketing independently
